Geospatial Analysis of South African Crime

Purpose

    To gain insight on the prominence of crime activities subject to the different provinces of South Africa. This is a project close to home, due to the simple fact that whilst being on my gap year in Australia I have been shocked at the amount of horrible things happening in my communities back home in South Africa. The project provides an eye opening representation of the severity of the issues we face as South Africans with regards to crime.

    Outcome

    The steps required to complete this project demanded a number of very important skills, starting with data collection by means of a tailored webscrapper built in Python. The geospatial data then needed to be fine tuned and prepared for analysis, which was conducted in a googlessheet allowing for simple reading of the dataset via R's googlessheet library. The visualisation dashboard was built using Shiny, providing a wide variety of approaches to visualise the data to the user. The structure of the application provides insight into the arrchitecture of how any basic application works in order to serve it's purpose, by adopting the structure of global functions, backend server and frontend ui.

Student Database Management System

Purpose

    To obtain a solution to a problem identified within my duties as a gap student in a Boarding house. I felt there was a necessity to gain a deeper insight into the academic struggles of our senior boarders heading into the exam preparation period. The initial database system was to merely add student data to a database. I then tailored the database to accommodate for our boarders, by identifying which subjects they had the least confidence in going into the exam preparation period. This allows us as a boarding staff to ensure that the optimal resources are provided to the boarders in the form of tutors whom have high compentency in the subject matter which our boarders struggle with the most.

    Outcome

    Not only do I feel that this was a great initiative to add value to my role as a gap student, but I believe this has been a tremendous learning experience. I have developed information systems leveraging tools such as SQL server and Visual Studio in the past, but this was fresh take with SQLite and Python, providing me with a new perspective in working with data. I thoroughly enjoyed the opportunity to take on this new challenge.
